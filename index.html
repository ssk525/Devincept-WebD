Sardar Vallav Bhai Patel

THE IRON MAN OF INDIA

Painting of Vallabhbhai Patel

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel is well known as a freedom fighter, political activist and lawyer.

  • Patel was born in Nadiad, Kheda district, and raised in the countryside of the state of Gujarat.
  • He served as the first Deputy Prime Minister of India from 1947 to 1950.
  • He was an Indian barrister and a senior leader of the Indian National Congress who played a leading role in the country's struggle for independence and guided its integration into a united, independent nation.
  • He was a successful lawyer. One of Mahatma Gandhi's earliest political lieutenants.
